What is Computer Ethics?

Computer ethics is a application of moral principles in the computer science world. Computer ethics can range from privacy rights all the way down how your program could effect peoples lives. In other words it is the etiquitte of the online world.

Why is ethics in CS important?

Just like in real life, ethics are a powerful tool in the computer science universe. Engineering in of itself is based around creating things that will help make peoples lives easier, hence the smartphone, self driving cars, clocks, social media, etc you name it are all made to uplift our qualities of life. Computer ethics help keep the CS world a moral place which is imparitive as all humans long to be safe.
